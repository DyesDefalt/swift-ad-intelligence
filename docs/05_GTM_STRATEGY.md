### Critical Pricing Changes dari Proposal Awal

**‚ùå JANGAN "unlimited" di managed tier.**
- Cost per generation (Vision API): ~Rp 1.000-5.000
- User dengan 500 generations: cost = Rp 500K-2.5M vs revenue Rp 149K = RUGI
- **Cap di 500/bulan.** Power users yang butuh lebih ‚Üí upgrade ke BYOK

- Ini menjaga recurring revenue stream

## 4. Launch Phases

### Phase 0: Pre-Launch (2 weeks before launch)
**Goal:** Build waiting list of 300+ people

**Actions:**
1. **Landing page live** ‚Äî Email capture + early access payment
2. **LinkedIn Content Series** ‚Äî "Building in Public"
   - Post 1: "Gw capek buka 5 tab buat bikin 1 iklan. Jadi gw bikin solusinya."
   - Post 2: "Ini yang terjadi kalau gw screenshot dashboard terus kasih ke AI..."
   - Post 3: "Demo pertama AdLens ‚Äî 30 detik dari gambar ke caption"
   - Post 4: "100 early access seats available. Here's why I'm charging Rp 299K not free."
3. **WhatsApp/Telegram Group** ‚Äî "AdLens Early Access Community"
   - Channel untuk beta testers + early customers
   - Direct feedback loop
4. **DM Outreach** ‚Äî 50 people dari network Dentsu/agency
   - "Gw bikin tool yang solve masalah gw sendiri. Mau coba?"

### Phase 1: Soft Launch (Week 1-2)
**Goal:** 50 paid users, 100+ installs

**Actions:**
1. Release via Developer Mode (beta testers get install guide)
2. Collect testimonials aggressively ‚Äî setiap user yang pakai, minta feedback
3. Fix critical bugs based on real usage
4. Create demo videos dari actual user workflows

### Phase 2: Public Launch (Week 3-4)
**Goal:** 200 paid users, 500+ installs

**Actions:**
1. Chrome Web Store submission (jika ready) ‚Äî $5 one-time fee
2. Full LinkedIn launch campaign
3. Short video demos (Reels/TikTok format) showing:
   - "Upload foto produk ‚Üí 5 caption dalam 10 detik"
   - "Screenshot dashboard ‚Üí insight lengkap"
4. Cross-post ke Twitter/X dan komunitas digital marketing Indonesia

### Phase 3: Growth (Month 2-6)
**Goal:** 500+ paid users, sustainable revenue

**Actions:**
1. **Content Marketing:** Blog/video tentang "cara analisis data campaign" menggunakan AdLens
2. **Community Building:** Active WhatsApp group, weekly tips
3. **Partnership:** Digital marketing bootcamps (RevoU, Purwadhika, Binar) ‚Äî mereka promote, students get discount
4. **Agency Sales:** Bulk license package untuk agency (5 seats = Rp 3.999.000)
5. **Referral Program:** Refer 1 friend = extend license 3 bulan gratis

### Secondary Channels (30% effort)

| Channel | Why | Content Type | Frequency |
|---------|-----|-------------|-----------|
| **Twitter/X** | #IndieHackers #MarTech community | Short demos, progress updates | 2-3x/week |
| **TikTok/Reels** | Demo visual yang catchy | 15-30s product demos | 1-2x/week |

### Channels to SKIP for now
- Facebook Groups (noisy, low-quality leads)
- SEO/Blog (takes 6+ months to rank, not worth for MVP)
- Paid Ads (don't spend money to acquire users until product-market fit confirmed)

## 8. Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Low initial adoption | Revenue < target | Pre-sell via waiting list before building |
